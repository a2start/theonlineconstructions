What is CSCS Red Experienced Technical, Supervisor, or Manager Card?

A CSCS Red Senior Technician, Supervisor, or Manager Card is a type of CSCS card that is issued to a Senior Technician, Supervisor, or Manager who is registered with NVQ or SVQ Level 3 or higher.

How should I renew my CSCS Red Experienced Technical, Supervisor, or Manager Card?

A person can only have this card once. Cardholders must complete their qualification prior to card expiration and apply for a qualified CSCS card.

How much time does it take to get a CSCS Red Experienced Technical, Supervisor, or Manager Card?

-After you successfully share the required documents for your CSCS Card via email, the Card will take 7-10 working days to deliver to your doorstep.

Why should I get the CSCS Card?

Across the UK, major construction industries require their employees to know and compete before they can start working anywhere. The CSCS provides such evidence by assessing the skills and competencies of representatives. Based on the skills of the representatives, they will be assigned the appropriate CSCS card so that they can be more active in their duties.

What to do in order to get the Red Experienced Technical, Supervisor, or Manager Card?

To obtain the Red Provisional Card, you must pass CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test (as per your Occupation), within the last 2 years.

What should I do, if my CSCS Red Experienced Technical, Supervisor, or Manager Card has been lost or stolen?

If your card is lost or stolen, you can order a replacement card for your CSCS Red Experienced Technical, Supervisor, or Manager Card. Fill out the application form or call us for Assistance and a physical copy will be delivered to your door within the next 7-10 business days.
