What is CSCS Black Manager Card?

CSCS Black Manager card is a type of CSCS card issued to a manager working on a construction site.

How should I renew my CSCS Black Manager Card?

To renew your CSCS Black Manager Card, you must have passed the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test for Managers & Professionals Test at your local examination center within the last 2 years. After passing the test, you can order a new CSCS Black Manager Card, valid for the next 5 years.

How much time does it take to get a CSCS, Black Manager Card?

Once the required CSCS card documents have been successfully shared via email, the card will be delivered to your door within 7-10 business days.

Why should I get the CSCS Card?

Across the UK, major construction industries require their employees to know and compete before they can start working anywhere. The CSCS provides such evidence by assessing the skills and competencies of representatives. Based on the skills of the representatives, they will be assigned the appropriate CSCS card so that they can be more active in their duties.

What to do in order to get the Black Manager Card?

To obtain the Black Manager Card, you must have passed the CITB Health, Safety and Environment Test for Managers and Professionals in the past 2 years and completed NVQ at Level 4 or above in Management.

What should I do, if my CSCS Black Manager Card has been lost or stolen?

If your card is lost or stolen, you can order a replacement card for your CSCS Black Manager card. Fill out the application form or call us for Assistance and a physical copy will be delivered to your door within the next 7-10 business days.
