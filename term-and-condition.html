For CITB Test

During online booking, we only take responsibility for arranging the test center and not the date & time selected by you, as test’s date & time are subject to change. Test can only be rescheduled or cancelled at least 4 days before the test date. If you fail to inform us in less than 4 days before the test date then your fee won’t get refunded.
If you avail our discount offer, then we’ll not provide any refund but you will still have the possibility to reschedule the test, provided that its being done at least 4 days before the test date. For test booking, we will be charging you £43 which includes test fee (£22.5) and our Administration fee. The charge may vary depending on the offer.
Those candidates who are not able to take the test or doesn’t qualify the 1st attempt, can go for a package we provide, i.e. “Test + Retake” which allows a candidate to take a 2nd attempt at a discounted price of £20. If you clear the test on the 1st attempt then the retake fees will not be refunded.
If you’re eligible for a refund, then it will be made within 12-15 working days. In case of cancellation of test 4 days before the test date, you'll be put through to a charge of administration/service fee and an amount of £22.5 will be refunded back to your account for the test. In case of cancellation of the “Test + Retake” package 4 days before the test date, you’ll be put through to a charge of administration/service fee and an amount of £35 will be refunded back to your account.
It is the candidate’s responsibility to ensure that he or she arrives at the Test Centre 15 minutes before the test time. CONSTRUCTION HELPS Ltd will accept no responsibility for candidates who arrive late or fail to arrive due to adverse weather conditions, failure of transport or any other circumstance. The candidate must carry a valid UK Driving License or Passport.
If You are not sure of which type or category of Test to be booked please contact CONSTRUCTION HELPS Ltd in the first instance by emailing us at info@constructionhelps.com or 0800 002 5614.
