Construction Health and Safety Courses

Nowadays, even the most qualified and well-trained professionals in construction and other fields fail to obtain jobs in prominent companies, thanks to the tough competition within the market. This doesn't mean that you should down tools when searching for a great job. Types of Construction Health and Safety Courses


One Day Health & Safety Awareness course - Required to get Green Labourer CSCS Card - Ideal for Labourers

Duration: 1 day
Description: Provides knowledge & skills in working at height, fire prevention & manual handling.

Apply Now
SSSTS (Site Supervisor Safety Training Scheme)

Duration: 2 day
Description: Provides basic information mainly on health and safety issues and helps to understand the legal responsibilities to work. Additionally, provides valuable support in safely managing a construction site.

Apply Now
Level 1 Award in Health and Safety in a Construction Environment

Duration: 1 day
Description: Provides knowledge about how to create a safe site for yourself and also others. This is aimed mainly at risk assessment for improved health & safety on work site.

Apply Now
SMSTS (Site Management Safety Training Scheme)

Duration: 5 day
Description: Provides overview of legislative changes and their impact in the workplace and information for management of occupational health.

Apply Now
