CITB Health, Safety And Environment Test

CITB TEST (Health Safety and Environment Test)

The Construction Industry Training Board(CITB) test is a major test that examines the knowledge of health and safety on a construction site. This test is the first step toward a CSCS card(Construction Skills Certification Scheme card). CITB test helps construction workers acquire knowledge on all the prevention of risk and safety at construction sites.
The Health safety and environment test offers multiple-choice questions and helps an individual become a qualified tradesman within the sector. This way they work on the sites safely and risk-free.
CITB Test can be given by anyone over the age of 16 years. This test consists of 50 questions and you get 45 minutes to answer these questions. All the questions are related to health and welfare, general safety, risk assessment and behavior, management of situations on construction sites, and case studies

Duration - 45 Minutes

Types of CITB Tests

Operative Tests mainly focus on the environment, health, management, legal aspects, high-risk activities, and safety.
The Specialist Test comprises multiple-choice questions and case studies. Choose and answer specialist questions on certain subjects:
  • Supervisory
  • Plumbing Or Gas
  • Tunneling
  • Highway Works
  • HVACR - Pipefitting And Welding
  • HVACR - Heating And Plumbing Services
  • HVACR - Refrigeration And Air Conditioning
  • HVACR - Ductwork
  • HVACR - Services And Facilities Maintenance
  • Demolition
  • Specialists Work At Height
  • Lifts And Escalators

Managers and Professionals Test consists of specialist knowledge questions on appropriate subjects along with multiple choice questions and case studies. These are:

  • Highway Works

Applicants need to pass the CITB test within 2 years before applying for a Trainee card. After passing the CITB test, you get a temporary red card that expires after 6 months.
CITB test is mainly given by General Labourers/Operatives. This test is necessary for the application for the CSCS Green labourer Card. It is mandatory to pass the CITB test to gain a CSCS card. CSCS cards are essential for entry on the majority of UK construction sites.
