Which Health, Safety & Environments Do I Need To Take?

You must complete the CITB, Health, safety and Environment Operatives Test to be eligible for a CSCS Green Card - This examination tests for the basic level of health, safety and environmental awareness. This test is suited for those looking to apply for the Green Labourer Card. You can order a revision book for this test to be in the with best chance of passing the test.
